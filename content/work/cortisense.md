# CortiSense

**Domain:** Predictive Maintenance, IoT  
**Geography:** Israel  
**Engagement type:** Value proposition · B2B pitch

---

## Background

CortiSense needed help proving to a multinational corporate its unique value proposition. Before we could find the right way to pitch, we needed to find out what it is exactly that we're pitching.

---

## The Process

A well-balanced team creates an interesting set of interviews, each drawing a different angle of the value proposition. After interviewing customers and digging into their competition, the answer became clear.

See [[process|The Process]] for how a typical engagement unfolds.

---

## Core Constraints Uncovered

**Healthy production** — Maintenance leaders are there to ensure that nothing deviates from perfect. Anything that can contribute to that beating rhythm of production will be in high demand.

**Least overhead** — Production, every manufacturer can tell you, is a matter of balance. A dance of machines and machinists to create something new. And if you can remove some of their burden, the better.
