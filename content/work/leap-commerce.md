# LEAP Commerce

**Domain:** eCommerce Enablement  
**Geography:** Singapore  
**Engagement type:** Investment pitch · Value strategy

---

## Background

LEAP was seeking a strategic investor to support its position in a highly competitive market. They had trouble convincing investors of their strong position. Their request was to polish that rough diamond into a compelling investment opportunity.

---

## The Process

Very often the first thing to do is to break everything down to pieces, and put them back together in an order that makes sense. With additional research and interviews with the company's leadership, their deal became clear.

See [[process|The Process]] for how a typical engagement unfolds.

---

## Core Constraints Uncovered

**Serviceability** — Serving larger chunks of the market grants customers the chance to win the minds and wallets of consumers all across the SEA region.

**Velocity** — Being able to master the eCommerce platforms and make the most out of them gives a brand the ability to experiment and adapt faster to shifting consumer demand.
